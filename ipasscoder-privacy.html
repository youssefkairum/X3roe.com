Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 22, 2025

01. Introduction

iPasscoder (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the App.

02. Data Collection

  • The App does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data with us or third parties.
  • All passcodes and sensitive information you store in the App are saved locally on your device only.

03. Data Security

  • Stored passcodes are encrypted using Apple’s CryptoKit (AES-GCM) to ensure confidentiality.
  • Exported backups are encrypted before leaving your device.
  • We do not have access to your encryption keys, passcodes, or backup files.

04. Purchases & Subscriptions

  • Premium features are purchased through Apple’s App Store in-app purchase system.
  • We do not process or store payment information.

05. User Control

  • You can edit or delete saved passcodes at any time with a paid subscription.
  • Deleting the App will remove all stored data from your device.

06. Children’s Privacy

iPasscoder is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

07. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be reflected in the App or on our website.
